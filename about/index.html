Why Choose Us?

What You Get?

Our main services are to provide standard processing of NGS data, tipically from raw data (FASTQ files) to called variants (VCF). We aim at doing it fast and with thorough QC throughout the all analyses.

We also offer tailored bioinformatic services, from specialized consulting, to finding biases in datasets.
